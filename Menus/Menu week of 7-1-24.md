Radish bean salad
1 rotisserie chicken
1 bag of persian cucumbers
1 bunch radishes
1 can chickpeas
1/4 red onion
5 green onions
1/4 minced cilantro
1 jalapeno
Dressing:
3 tbsp minced dill
1/2 cup greek yogurt
2 tbsp red wine vinegar
juice of 2 lemons
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp sea salt

- Cut up rotisserie chicken - let juices drain
- chop cucumber into small pieces
- chop radishes into small pieces

- pour into bowl
