# Black pepper stir fried noodles (easy)


[4CE90F1B-A335-4F93-956F-46BB9C628C39](attachments/4CE90F1B-A335-4F93-956F-46BB9C628C39.jpeg)

Sauce: (doubled from original recipe, which is necessary)
- [ ] 6 tablespoons dark soy sauce (see FAQs if using regular soy sauce)
- [ ] 2 tablespoon rice vinegar
- [ ] 4 tablespoons oyster sauce
- [ ] 3 tablespoons brown sugar

Udon Stir Fry:
- [ ] a little bit of neutral oil
- [ ] A whole bag of stir fry veggies
- [ ] two 7-ounce packages of udon noodles (the cooked, fat, squishy ones – see FAQs)

Finishing Touches:
- [ ] 1–2 cloves fresh garlic, minced
- [ ] a ton of freshly ground black pepper
