# Smoothies

Single serving recipes

**Healthy Chocolate Blueberry Smoothie**
* 1/2 banana frozen
* 3/4 cup blueberries frozen
* 2 Tbsp cacao powder
* 1 Tbsp almond butter
* 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
* 1 scoop vanilla protein powder (could also use chocolate)
* 1 cup baby spinach fresh
* 3/4 cup unsweetened almond milkmore if needed
* 2 Tbsp granola optional for topping

**Strawberry Vanilla or Chocolate Smoothie**

* 1 cup frozen strawberries
* 1/3 banana
* 1 cup riced cauliflower
* 2 pitted dates
* 1 Tbsp creamy almond butter
* 1/3 cup plain Greek yogurt
* 1 scoop Clean Simple Eats Protein
* 1/4 tsp cinnamon (optional)
* 1.5 cups unsweetened almond milk

**Chocolate cherry smoothie**

* 1/2 cup frozen cherries *(about 10)*1 cup creamy oat milk
* 1 tablespoon almond butter
* half a frozen banana
* a handful of spinach *(1/4 cup packed)*1 scoop vanilla protein powder
* 2 tablespoons cocoa powder 


Blend on high for 2-3 minutes making sure there are no chunks.
